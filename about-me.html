Hey, Folks!

My name is Sanil Jacob. I’m a Product designer based in Kerala, India. I’m passionate about solving problems through design thinking. As a teenager at school, I was the kid other students approached about issues with their phone or laptop.

I was the go-to guy for fixing any technical problems. Today, not much has changed. I love solving problems. I'm learning new things every day.

I enjoy working with people who value aesthetics, who understand the power of simplicity and who know that a good customer experience is paramount. By keeping your identity at the heart of my approach, my goal is to find the simplest solution to meet your needs.
